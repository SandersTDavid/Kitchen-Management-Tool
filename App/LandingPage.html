Student Project UoP

This website was produced for the purpose of a university software engineering project. Please only use this website for experimentation with your training subject.

How can your kitchen benefit

Using these tools, you could reduce the time it takes to fully train a chef on multiple sections in the kitchen.

Reduce the amount of training time and save on staff expenses.

Employees that are happier with their training are more likely to lead to staff retention.

Why use iCanPrep

This website provides useful tools to help new or inexperienced chefs to get to know their section in the kitchen.

The first tool on the components page allows the chef to take out all the different components in the section and to indivdually add them to a list along with a method of preparation and the amount of time it should take to prepare.

The second tool on the preparation list page allows the chef to quickly add an item to a daily preplist and swap the order of it. This helps the chef think about the best order that the list could go in to get the most amount of work done. It will also display the total preparation time.
